Journalism

Argos - Secondary ticketing (Dutch)

As a freelancer I produced an episode for the investigative radio program Argos (VPRO), about the reselling of concert tickets and how this market evolved in the Netherlands. The research for this project was done jointly with Atze de Vrieze of 3voor12 (VPRO).

For this project I researched, edited the audio, recorded and conducted interviews.

VPRO Dorst - De Nieuwe Laura (Dutch)

VPRO Dorst enabled me to take part in a radio storytelling workshop, taught by the makers of Toendra and Plots. For this workshop I made several short radio documentaries. The workshop ended with a listening night at the Volkshotel in Amsterdam, for which I made this radio documentary about a young woman and her journey with MS.

For this project I recorded and conducted the interview and also edited the audio.

Web Development

Stick to it - app

In the last two weeks of the Le Wagon coding bootcamp I coded this web application in a team. It is designed to help you focus on life goals like being healthier, being more zen, being more productive. To make progress on these life goals, you can take on 21 day challenges (for example: 21 days of yoga). For these challenges you can buddy up with another user of the app to motivate each other.
